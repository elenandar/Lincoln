  RUSSIAN TIME ANCHOR FIX - DEMONSTRATION
=================================================================================

This demonstrates the fix for the TimeEngine bug where Russian relative time
expressions were not being detected, causing time to advance by only the
generic scene rate instead of the intended time jump.

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 1: "Спустя 2 часа пришла Хлоя" (After 2 hours, Chloe arrived)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BEFORE THE FIX:
  Initial time: Day 1, 8:00 AM
  Input: "Спустя 2 часа пришла Хлоя"
  ❌ Time advanced by: ~10 minutes (generic scene rate)
  ❌ Final time: Day 1, 8:10 AM
  ❌ Expected: Day 1, 10:00 AM
  PROBLEM: Russian anchor not detected, user context lost!

AFTER THE FIX:
  Initial time: Day 1, 8:00 AM
  Input: "Спустя 2 часа пришла Хлоя"
  ✅ Time advanced by: 120 minutes (2 hours)
  ✅ Final time: Day 1, 10:00 AM
  ✅ Russian anchor detected and applied correctly!

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 2: "Через три минуты раздался звонок" (In three minutes, a call came)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

AFTER THE FIX:
  Initial time: Day 1, 10:00 AM
  Input: "Через три минуты раздался звонок"
  ✅ Time advanced by: 3 minutes
  ✅ Final time: Day 1, 10:03 AM
  ✅ Word numeral "три" correctly parsed to 3!

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
SCENARIO 3: "Через пару часов все собрались" (In a couple of hours, everyone gathered)
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

AFTER THE FIX:
  Initial time: Day 1, 10:03 AM
  Input: "Через пару часов все собрались в спортзале"
  ✅ Time advanced by: 120 minutes (2 hours)
  ✅ Final time: Day 1, 12:03 PM
  ✅ Colloquial expression "пару" correctly interpreted as 2!

---------------------------------------------------------------------------------
BACKWARD COMPATIBILITY: English patterns still work

  ✅ Time advanced by: 120 minutes
  ✅ No regression - English anchors work perfectly!
